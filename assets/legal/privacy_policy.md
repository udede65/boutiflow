# Privacy Policy for RoomPilot

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2024

## 1. Introduction
RoomPilot ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Data Collection & Storage
**Local-First Architecture:**
RoomPilot is designed as an offline-first application. By default, all data you enter (guest names, bookings, revenue) is stored **locally on your device**. We do not have access to this data.

**Cloud Backup (Premium):**
If you subscribe to RoomPilot Premium and enable Cloud Backup, your data will be encrypted and synchronized with our secure cloud servers (powered by Supabase). This allows you to restore your data on other devices. You can delete this cloud data at any time via the App settings.

## 3. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** If you create an account for Cloud Backup, we collect your email address.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., app crashes, feature usage) to improve the App.
- **Payment Information:** All payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not store your credit card details.

## 4. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the App.
- To sync your data across devices (if Premium).
- To improve our services and fix bugs.

## 5. Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights
- **Access & Delete:** You have the right to access your data and request its deletion. Since most data is stored locally, you can simply delete the App to remove local data. For cloud data, use the "Delete Account" option in Settings.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@roompilot.app
