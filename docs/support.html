Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by tapping "Forgot Password" on the login screen. An email will be sent to you with instructions.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! BoutiFlow is designed to be offline-first. Your reservations and data are saved locally and synced when you reconnect to the internet.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Go to the "Settings" menu inside the app and tap on "Upgrade to Premium". You can choose between a monthly or annual subscription securely via your App Store account.

Can I generate a police report?

Yes. You can generate a PDF or Excel document containing the daily guest list directly from the app, which you can then manually upload to the relevant local authorities.
